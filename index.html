How's This Book?

“How’s This book?” is a website with a database served through MySQL in conjunction with Node.js that allows Oregon State University students (approximately 20,000 Users total) to publish Ratings for Books . The interface of the website will also allow Users to access details of Books , such as their Authors and Genres . The primary function of the website is to provide Users with a hub to store and access their Ratings for Books .
